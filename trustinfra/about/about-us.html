About Us

INTRODUCTION

THE GENESIS

Established in 1972 in Abu Dhabi, UAE - TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING is one of the leading and fastest-growing Contracting Companies in the MENA region

THE LEGACY

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING has completed multi-disciplined projects (including, but not limited to) the design and building of Skyscrapers, Mixed-use Developments, Residential and Commercial Communities, IT Data Centres, Field Hospitals, Specialized ETFE, Indoor Intelligent Farming Structures (including plantations) and Infrastructure Development

THE SCOPE

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING is a qualified Fast Track Design & Build Contractor, providing end-to-end Construction solutions through turnkey projects

THE REACH

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING's scope of operations span Asia and Africa, with varied contracting operations throughout the MENA region

THE TRUST

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING is a recognized and accredited ISO-certified Company.

THE CLIENTELE

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING's esteemed clients include Government Institutions, Private Developers and Investors

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING Profile

We Are Expert In All
Industry Works

TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING owns and manages a diversified business portfolio, involved in construction, automotive supply and services. Since its foundation, TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING has been dedicated to providing the highest quality of products and services through a committed, professional management and staff.
TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING began in 1972 and started with a small fleet of trucks and equipment, serving the Abu Dhabi Municipality. Today, TRUST INFRA GENERAL TRANSPORTING AND CONTRACTING has more than 4 main branches in Abu Dhabi only in addition to the Headquarter which located in Abu Dhabi too.
The Company continues to grow, and is always looking for new opportunities. Its priority is to add value to its many customers and to the local communities where it operates.
